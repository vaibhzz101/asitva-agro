🚀 EmailJS Setup Guide for Astitva Agro

📧 Step 1: Create EmailJS Account

  1. Go to EmailJS.com
  2. Click "Sign Up"
  3. Create account with your email
  4. Verify your email address

⚙️ Step 2: Add Email Service

  1. Go to "Email Services" in dashboard
  2. Click "Add New Service"
  3. Choose "Gmail" (recommended)
  4. Service ID: service_astitva
  5. Connect your Gmail account
  6. Click "Create Service"

📝 Step 3: Create Email Template

  1. Go to "Email Templates"
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Template ID: template_contact
  4. Copy this template content:
Subject: 🌾 New Inquiry from {{from_name}} - Astitva Agro Dear Astitva Agro Team, You have received a new contact form submission from your website: 📋 CONTACT DETAILS: 👤 Name: {{from_name}} 📧 Email: {{from_email}} 📱 Phone: {{phone}} 🏢 Company: {{company}} 🌾 Product Interest: {{product_interest}} 💬 MESSAGE: {{message}} 📅 Submitted: {{timestamp}} --- Please respond directly to: {{reply_to}} Best regards, Astitva Agro Website System

To Email: info@astitvaagrofpcl.com

From Name: Astitva Agro Website

Reply To: {{reply_to}}

🔑 Step 4: Get Your Keys

  1. Go to "Account" → "General"
  2. Copy your Public Key
  3. Note down:
    • Service ID: service_astitva
    • Template ID: template_contact
    • Public Key: YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY

🔧 Step 5: Update Your Code

Replace YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY in the API route with your actual public key from EmailJS.

// In app/api/contact/route.ts user_id: "YOUR_ACTUAL_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE"

✅ Step 6: Test Your Form

  1. Save and deploy your website
  2. Fill out the contact form
  3. Check your email at info@astitvaagrofpcl.com
  4. You should receive the formatted email!

📊 EmailJS Dashboard Features

  • Email History: See all sent emails
  • Usage Stats: Track monthly usage
  • Free Tier: 200 emails/month
  • Delivery Status: Monitor success rates

🛠️ Troubleshooting

  • Emails not sending? Check your Public Key
  • Wrong template? Verify Service ID and Template ID
  • Gmail issues? Make sure Gmail service is connected
  • Spam folder? Check spam/junk folder

🎉 You're All Set!

Your contact form will now send emails directly to info@astitvaagrofpcl.com whenever someone submits the form on your website.

What happens next:

  • User fills form → Clicks "Send Message"
  • Email sent instantly to your inbox
  • User sees success message
  • You can reply directly to customer's email
